# Export Checklist

- Chapter argument is coherent from start to end.
- Repeated ideas have been merged or removed.
- Terminology is stable.
- Unsupported claims are not strengthened.
- Figure references and placeholders are consistent.
- Heading hierarchy is clean.
- Export draft reads like chapter prose, not notes.
- PDF filename is stable and descriptive.
- PDF is written to `output/pdfs/`.
- Final rendering is readable and shareable.

# Chapter Output Map

## Default outputs

- `manuscript/sections/<chapter_name>.md`
  - merged chapter working draft
- `manuscript/sections/<chapter_name>_export.md`
  - polished export version
- `manuscript/sections/<chapter_name>_export.html`
  - optional intermediate for PDF conversion
- `output/pdfs/<chapter_name>.pdf`
  - final single-chapter PDF

## Interpretation

- The working draft should remain editable and traceable.
- The export draft should be cleaner and more publication-oriented.
- The PDF is the shareable artifact for review.

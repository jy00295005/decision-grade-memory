## Why the new version is closer to a strong conference-paper method section

- The section now centers on one claim and repeats it only when strategically necessary.
- Subsections are shorter and each performs a distinct methodological function.
- Figures are allowed to carry more of the motivational and structural burden, reducing redundant prose.
- The representation, framework, and scope are retained, but explanatory padding and design-note language have been removed.
- The resulting section reads more like a narrow, defensible conference-paper method than like internal design documentation.

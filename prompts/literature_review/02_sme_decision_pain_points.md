# Literature Review Prompt 02

## Purpose

Narrow the review to practical decision-making pain points for SME owners and operators.

## Prompt

```md
Continue the literature review, but now narrow the focus to the actual decision-making pain points of SME owners and operators.

Research focus:
What makes decision-making difficult for SMEs in practice, especially under uncertainty, resource constraints, fragmented data, and fast-changing operating environments?

Tasks:
1. Identify the most recurring categories of SME decision pain points in the literature.
2. Distinguish between:
   - data problems
   - operational problems
   - strategic problems
   - organizational/cognitive problems
3. Pay special attention to papers discussing:
   - incomplete information
   - fragmented systems
   - cash flow pressure
   - staffing constraints
   - dynamic market conditions
   - weak analytical capacity
   - informal decision processes
4. Create a new note:
   `references/notes/sme_decision_pain_points.md`

Use this structure:
- Executive summary
- Pain point taxonomy
- What is specific to SMEs vs larger firms
- Evidence-backed observations
- Claims needing more evidence
- Implications for AI support system design

Requirements:
- Do not invent references.
- Keep claims grounded.
- Use TODO markers where the literature is thin.
- Prefer synthesis over long summaries.
```

## Notes

- Use after the broad scan.
- This prompt isolates the user/problem side of the paper.

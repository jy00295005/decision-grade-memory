# Literature Review Prompt 04

## Purpose

Synthesize the review notes into a conservative research-positioning document.

## Prompt

```md
Synthesize the literature findings into a research-positioning note.

Inputs:
- `references/notes/sme_ai_decision_review_round1.md`
- `references/notes/sme_ai_decision_review_matrix.md`
- `references/notes/sme_decision_pain_points.md`
- `references/notes/ai_limits_for_sme_decision_support.md`

Task:
Create a new note at `docs/research_positioning.md`

Structure:
- Problem framing
- Why SME decision support is hard
- Why current AI support is insufficient
- Candidate research gap
- Competing framings of the gap
- Strongest version of the scenario-memory thesis
- Weakest / most defensible version of the thesis
- What would count as evidence
- What would count as non-evidence
- 3 candidate paper contribution statements
- 3 candidate paper titles

Requirements:
- Keep evidence-backed synthesis separate from conceptual framing.
- Do not invent references.
- Be conservative.
- Prefer a narrow, defensible positioning over a broad but weak one.
```

## Notes

- Use after the core literature notes already exist.
- This is the bridge from literature review to paper framing.
